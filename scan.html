Camera Access and Capture Photo

If you denied camera access, follow these steps:

  • 1. Make sure you are using a modern browser like Chrome or Firefox.
  • 2. Look for a camera permission prompt in the browser's address bar.
  • 3. Click the camera icon and select "Allow" for camera access.
  • 4. If no prompt appears, you may need to enable camera permissions in your browser's settings.
  • 5. For mobile devices, check your phone's privacy settings to allow camera access for this site.
